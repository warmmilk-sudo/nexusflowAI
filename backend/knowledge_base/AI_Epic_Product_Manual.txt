# AI Epic™ Co-Ablation System - Product Manual

## Product Overview
The AI Epic™ Co-Ablation System represents a breakthrough in minimally invasive tumor treatment, combining advanced cryoablation and thermal therapy technologies. This dual-modality approach provides superior treatment outcomes for various tumor types across multiple medical specialties.

## Key Features

### Dual-Modality Technology
- **Cryoablation**: Ultra-low temperature freezing (-196°C) using liquid nitrogen
- **Thermal Therapy**: Precise heating (60-100°C) for complete tumor destruction
- **Sequential Treatment**: Automated switching between freeze and heat cycles

### Advanced Imaging Integration
- **Real-time CT Guidance**: Continuous visualization during procedure
- **MRI Compatibility**: Compatible with 1.5T and 3.0T MRI systems
- **Ultrasound Integration**: Multi-modal imaging support
- **3D Treatment Planning**: Pre-procedure planning software included

### Clinical Applications

#### Urology Applications
- **Prostate Cancer**: Focal therapy for localized tumors
- **Renal Cell Carcinoma**: Nephron-sparing treatment option
- **Bladder Tumors**: Minimally invasive alternative to surgery
- **Treatment Benefits**: Reduced recovery time, preserved organ function

#### Interventional Radiology
- **Liver Tumors**: Primary and metastatic lesions
- **Lung Nodules**: Peripheral lung cancer treatment
- **Bone Metastases**: Pain management and tumor control
- **Soft Tissue Masses**: Various benign and malignant lesions

#### Oncology Applications
- **Multi-organ Capability**: Liver, lung, kidney, bone, soft tissue
- **Palliative Care**: Pain relief for advanced cancer patients
- **Combination Therapy**: Compatible with chemotherapy and radiation
- **Repeat Treatments**: Multiple sessions as needed

## Technical Specifications

### System Components
- **Control Console**: Touch-screen interface with AI-guided protocols
- **Cryoprobes**: Multiple sizes (1.7mm, 2.4mm, 3.0mm diameter)
- **Thermal Probes**: Adjustable heating elements
- **Liquid Nitrogen Supply**: Integrated cooling system
- **Safety Monitoring**: Real-time temperature and pressure monitoring

### Performance Metrics
- **Freeze Zone**: Up to 4cm diameter ice ball
- **Heating Zone**: Precise 1-3cm thermal ablation
- **Treatment Time**: 15-45 minutes depending on tumor size
- **Accuracy**: Sub-millimeter precision with image guidance

## Competitive Advantages

### vs. Traditional Argon-Helium Systems
- **Superior Cooling**: Liquid nitrogen provides faster, more uniform freezing
- **Dual Modality**: Combined freeze-heat cycle ensures complete cell death
- **Cost Efficiency**: Lower operating costs, no expensive gas cartridges
- **Reliability**: Fewer mechanical components, reduced maintenance

### vs. Radiofrequency Ablation
- **Larger Treatment Zone**: Single probe covers larger area
- **Better Visualization**: Ice ball clearly visible on imaging
- **Less Pain**: Cryoablation provides natural anesthesia
- **Reduced Complications**: Lower risk of bleeding and infection

### vs. Microwave Ablation
- **Precise Control**: Better temperature control and monitoring
- **Dual Mechanism**: Freeze-thaw-heat cycle more effective
- **Versatility**: Suitable for more tumor types and locations
- **Safety Profile**: Lower risk of thermal injury to adjacent organs
